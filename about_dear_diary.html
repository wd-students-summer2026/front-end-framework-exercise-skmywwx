Frequently Asked Questions

We aim to provide you with a joyful experience to record your precious memories. Use this website to note down your memories!
Your expression is not limited! Express as freely as you can!
Our primary products include a website version and a mobile version of "Dear Diary". The functions available on the webstie version and the mobile version are the same: creators create their personal diaries, and they can either share the content to public or maintain private. We value every creator's privacy!
You can create however you want, either such as texts and visuals! The created diaries will be automatically stored according to the first confirmed date of submission, you can either choose to renew the date or keep the original date during later edits. If you want, feel free to change the items' placement on the timeline.

Afterall, there's only one main goal: Express your imagination and store your prescious memories!
